ABOUT US

Empowering the communities
Enriching lives.

A community aid and development agency that aims at empowering the communites at the grassroot level and enriching lives positively

Our Vision

To build a united, inclusive, and empowered society where all individuals have equal access to education, healthcare,human rights, and opportunities for a dignified life.

Our Mission

To uplift vulnerable communities by promoting education, improving health, alleviating poverty,advancing human rights, and empowering persons with disabilities.

Our Programs

Education

Giving bursaries of KSh 5,000 to learners from marginalized families, helping them access quality education for future prosperity.

Health

Supporting mental health awareness and assisting community members with hospital bills.

Gender Based Violence (GBV)

Championing human rights and fighting against FGM to protect girls.

Economic Empowerment

Training members in entrepreneurship and providing KSh 5,000 grants to boost their businesses.
